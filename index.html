About Me

I build AI systems for robots that work alongside people in real environments. My focus is on making robots that can see what they are doing, understand their own motion, and communicate their intent clearly enough for humans to trust them.

My graduate research at ASU asks whether frontier AI models like GPT and Gemini can automatically judge how clearly a robot moves, replacing the need for human observers. My thesis was approved April 2026. I also have hands-on experience in LLM alignment (DPO, RLHF), diffusion model training, GPU-scale ML pipelines (A100, CUDA), and mechanical engineering (CAD, FEA, manufacturing).

Thesis Research

MS Thesis · Arizona State University · Approved April 2026

Vision-Language Models as Proxies for Human Judgment of Robot Motion Legibility

🎓 Advisor: Prof. Nakul Gopalan 🤖 LOGOS Robotics Lab, ASU 👥 Committee: Hani ben Amor · Kunal Garg

Can AI models like Gemini, GPT, or Claude watch a robot move and judge whether its intent is clear — replacing human observers? This thesis builds that system and extends it to guide trajectory selection inside diffusion policies.

  • VLM goal-inference pipeline using cumulative prefix-frame prompting — tested on 8 robot manipulation scenarios (legible and ambiguous variants), structured JSON outputs (pA, pB, visual cue, legibility flag)
  • Behavior-conditioned diffusion policy (DDPM + classifier-free guidance, λ=2.0) trained across legibility, predictability, safety, and grounding behaviors in PyBullet on a simulated Franka Panda arm
  • 5-stage Best-of-K trajectory ranking: generate K candidates → render frames → VLM score → re-execute best; legibility improves monotonically with K

VLM Best-of-K Trajectory Ranking for Diffusion Policies

MS Thesis · LOGOS Robotics Lab, ASU · 2025–2026

Behavior-conditioned diffusion policy (DDPM + classifier-free guidance) generates K candidate trajectories via different noise seeds. Each is rendered to frames and scored by a VLM across legibility, predictability, safety, and grounding. The highest-scoring candidate is re-executed.
